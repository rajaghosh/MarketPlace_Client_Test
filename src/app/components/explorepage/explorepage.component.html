FAQ

How do I change my credit card details?
Click Login -> Enter your email -> You will get OTP on your email -> After login please click ‘change card details’ -> Enter new card details then save.

How to change subscription product?
Click Login -> Enter your email -> You will get OTP on your email -> After login please click ‘update product subscription’ -> Modify product and click save.

How to get free visit?
Customers need to use same email address to login Telehealth portal. Procedure or Visit charges will be waives off. If you are still experiencing any problems, then please contact patienthelpdesk@drstellamd.com

How to cancel my subscription?
Click Login -> Enter your email -> You will get OTP on your email -> After login please click the ‘Stop Subscription’ button.
